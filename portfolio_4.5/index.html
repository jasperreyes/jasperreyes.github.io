Who the hell am I?
Hello. I'm Jasper.

I enjoy designing the human experience, the convergence of digital tools and techniques with analog craft, patterns, modularity, geometry, simplicity, capturing the world in photos and in memories, examining the mundane, snowboarding, and sushi.

I studied architecture and industrial design and worked as a furniture designer. I'm now taking my experience in crafting space, flow, and comfort to design experiences and interfaces.

Thanks for stopping by!
