Amazing coffee & food are our speciality.


We're proud to serve FairTrade & Organic Colectivo coffee, FairTrade & Organic Rishi Tea, and Sassy Cow Creamery & Sugar River Diary milk, cream, yogurt and ice cream (don't worry, we've got soy & almond milk too)! Our baristas are Colectivo trained and Captain Kate certified. Your drink is going to be the best around... period.


We make all of our sauces, dips, dressings and soups from scratch right here in the Paradigm kitchen. We source fresh and local ingredients first, including veggies from our own garden! We personally pick up our Ciabatta bread by bicycle from City Bakery everyday! Our dairy & eggs come from ethically sound farms that we have visited personally. It's a lot of work but we like to think all of that love makes the difference!

Booking


If you're in the area, stop by our open mic (7pm every third Sunday of the month). Otherwise, drop us a line if you'd like to play at Paradigm! Tell us the date/alternate dates you are hoping for along with your name and genre in the subject line. Be sure to send us a link to your music and a brief description of your band and setup.

We receive hundreds of emails, so patience Grasshopper! Be assured that we review every submission personally and we will contact you if we are interested in having you play. Please no phone calls unless we have you booked and on the calendar!

FAQ if you have been booked:

Shows start @ 8pm (unless otherwise agreed upon), please be loaded in no later than 7:30pm in time for a proper soundcheck. We have a 16-channel Carvin board, 2 speakers, 2 monitors and all the fixins. There is an upright piano available onstage, if you need anything else, bring it with you! You will have a sound engineer behind the board. Drummers - We love you, so please don’t suck. We can accommodate most drums, but be prepared to play to the room and just in case bring those hotrods and/or brush sticks! We’ll pass a tip jar while you perform. We do our best to promote our performers and we hope you do the same! Get the word out, promote yourself locally with posters and press releases!

ReBike



ReBike is housed in Paradigm’s basement, everyone in the community is welcome to come down on Wednesday evenings for a bicycle repair workshop. If you volunteer for a few hours, you get a bike! Yes, it’s that easy! We are proud to announce that ReBike has surpassed the marker of putting over 1,000 bicycles back on the road.

Paradigm, in cooperation with EarthFest, Rebike and WorkingBikes Cooperative of Chicago holds a bike drive every August! All the bikes donated will replenish ReBike’s stock and also Working Bikes’ in Chicago who send bicycles to rural and impoverished communities in Latin America and Africa! We’d like to thank everyone for their continued support of Rebike through donations!! We’ve collected nearly 200 bicycles during this event alone!
