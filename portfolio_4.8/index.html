Hi. My name is Jasper Reyes.
I'm a designer based in Chicago.
Who am I?

I'm a designer of mixed creative background. I studied architecture and industrial design and professionally designed furniture and lighting for the home and medical industries. I've found my calling and I'm now taking my experiences of designing space, flow, and comfort to design digital interfaces.

I enjoy designing and improving the human experience, the convergence of digital tools and techniques with analog craft, capturing the world in photos and in memories, patterns, modularity, geometry, simplicity, examining the mundane, snowboarding, and sushi.

Thanks for stopping by!
