Hi. My name is Jasper Reyes.
I'm a designer based in Chicago.
Who am I?

I'm a designer of mixed creative background. I studied to become an architect and industrial designer. I've professionally designed furniture and lighting for the home and medical industries. I'm now taking my experiences of designing space, flow, and comfort to design digital interfaces.

I enjoy designing and improving the human experience, the convergence of digital tools and techniques with analog craft, capturing the world in photos and in memories, patterns, modularity, geometry, simplicity, examining the mundane, snowboarding, and sushi.

Thanks for stopping by!

Hydratile


Hydratile is a modular ceramic tile that creates a multitude of graphic patterns and spatial forms to evoke literal and symbolic imagery of water.
The task was to use ceramic slip-casting techniques to design an object.

Find Karan


This game uses audio feedback as a wayfinding tool for the user to reach the hidden target. It's a parody of an existing game that makes light of apologetic Canadian stereotypes as exemplified by our Canadian classmate, Karan. Conceived by the Product Track at Startup Institute, it was built with his blessing without using the original source code by myself and Tom Benneche.
