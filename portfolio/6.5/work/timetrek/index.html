User Personas

A wide range of visitors could feasibly visit the Time Trek portal, each with their own needs and motives. Conducting this thought experience helped develop a list of potential features for the portal.

Existing Booking Site Research

While fictional, Time Trek has many functional parallels to existing travel booking websites, which were used to understand common UI conventions, features, etc.

Information Architecture

With research from user personas and reviewing existing travel booking websites, the list of potential features was then arranged into logical groupings and workflows.

Wireframes & Prototyping

I then explored a multitude of interface possibilities using hand sketches, Sketch, and Invision.

Visual Design

Several branding options were explored and ultimately the rugged, archeological, yet streamlined feel was chosen.

Content Design

For realism, faux information was important to include. However, I wanted to include references of time travel pop culture, which manifested in many ways including names of places, people, etc.
