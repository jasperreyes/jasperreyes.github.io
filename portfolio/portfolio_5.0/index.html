Who am I?



I'm a designer and front-end developer based in the Chicago area.   Studying architecture and industrial design was the start of my design foundation. I then designed professionally for the medical and residential furniture industries which expanded my understanding of scale, flow, comfort, working with clients, and manufacturing.

I wanted combine my design experiences thus far with a growing interest in user experience and coding and I now live to create solutions and imagine new possibilities in the world of web and interfaces.

Have a look around. If you have any questions, don't be a stranger :-) Thanks for stopping by!
