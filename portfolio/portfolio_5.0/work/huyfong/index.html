Huy Fong Foods


Huy Fong Foods, the company behind the popular Sriracha sauce with the rooster logo, has the opportunity to improve its online presence by engaging with it's growing fan base and refreshing the look and feel of the current website, while keeping the site low-maintenace for it's administrators.

*This project is not sponsored or paid for by the Huy Fong Foods Company, but is a self-initiated project.

** project currently in progress **

Market Leader

Existing Website

The current website excels in its simplicty, but lacks in its engagement of Huy Fong Sriracha fans. Additionally, the current visual styling hasn't aged well and doesn't account for mobile viewing.

Current Huy Fong Foods Website


User Personas

In order to empathize with potential users of the updated website, user personas helped to discover possible needs and use cases that the site may cater to.


Information Architecture

Based on prior research and personas, the site's possible contents and the flow of those contents were carefully considered and explored in many configurations, seeking an intuitive, organized, and logical flow.


Wireframes

With the information architecture as a guide, the wireframes aimed to create a clean, interesting flow of information with respect to the viewer's screen, whether desktop or mobile.


Mockups

Based on the wireframing, the mockups address visual cues and how the user interacts with the site.


The New Huy Fong Website

Though still under construction, the responsive structure, navigation menus, and pages have been built out.
