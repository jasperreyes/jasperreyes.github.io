Find Karan


This game uses audio feedback based on your cursor's location on screen to engage and help the player find an invisible object. The main character is based on Karan, a fellow designer, who became known for fulfilling the Canadian stereotype of excessively apologizing

The game works optimally in the Chrome browser. Special thanks to Tom Benneche for his expertise in writing the JavaScript location randomizer.
