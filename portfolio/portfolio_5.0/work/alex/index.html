Corporal Alexander Pierog


Corporal Alexander Pierog was a photographer for the US Army Military Police from 1942 to 1945. He documented both official events and personal experiences during his service while training in California, at sea, and while stationed in Australia, New Guinea, and the Philippines.

Combining strong personal interests in both history and photography, and with the blessing and generosity of his surviving wife, I was given the privilege of viewing his work and documenting it digitally to preserve and share it with the world.
