About me

Hey! As a kid, I spent countless hours with my brother and a big red box filled with Legos. Every day, I imagined and built newer and better worlds. This was the seed that led to a formal design foundation in architecture and industrial design. My understanding of structure, organization, user flow, and user needs along with shifts in technology and opportunities led me to the worlds of product and user experience design. Now, my big red box is the collection of tools, skills, experiences I've picked up along the way.
