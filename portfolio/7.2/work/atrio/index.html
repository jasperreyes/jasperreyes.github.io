Atrio

Atrio, a video production startup, needed an easy-to-navigate website to showcase their work and the company. Characteristic of their video work, the site errs on the side of simplicity through consistent design language and minimal, but deliberate use of color.

Information architecture & wireframing

With Atrio, we assessed the needs and purposes for which the website would be designed including general information about the company, their work, process, and how to get in touch. Wireframes allowed us to explore layout possibilities.

Medium-fidelity mockups

Medium-Fidelity mockups further explored layout and color, which helped Atrio decide on a relatively neutral colors to maximize the visual impact of their presented videos.
